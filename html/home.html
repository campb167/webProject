OUR COMPANY

RAZORLIGHT

We launched Razorlight Games in 2006 to develop, publish, and support games made by players, for players. In 2009, we released our debut title, Rainbow Six: Seige. Rainbow has gone on to be the most-played PC game in the world and a key driver of the explosive growth of esports. Players are the foundation of our community and it’s for them that we continue to evolve the Rainbow experience both in and out of game. Founded by Finlay Campbell, and currently under the leadership of CEO Taine Anderson, we're headquartered in Leamington Spa, Warwickshite, and have 2,500+ staff in 20+ offices worldwide.
